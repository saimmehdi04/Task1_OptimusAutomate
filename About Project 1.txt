📊 Project 2 Completed: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Report 🚀

Excited to share that I have successfully completed my second Data Analytics project as part of my learning journey!

In this project, I performed Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on an e-commerce dataset to uncover meaningful insights, identify trends, and understand customer purchasing behavior.

🔍 Key Tasks Performed:

✅ Data Quality Check (Missing Values, Duplicates, Invalid Dates)
✅ Descriptive Statistics (Mean, Median, Count, Min, Max)
✅ Product Performance Analysis
✅ Revenue Analysis by Product Category
✅ Order Status Analysis
✅ Payment Method Analysis
✅ Trend Analysis by Year
✅ Key Business Insights & Observations

📈 Key Findings:

• Average order value: $1,053.97
• Customers purchase approximately 3 items per order on average
• Printers, Chairs, and Laptops generated the highest revenue
• Online payments were the most preferred payment method
• Revenue showed a slight decline from 2023 to 2025
• High cancellation and return rates indicate areas for business improvement
